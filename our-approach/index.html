Our Approach

Proactive + Connected for Real Business

At Proactive Accounting & Tax, our approach is simple:
plan ahead, stay connected, and eliminate surprises.

Most accounting stress comes from reactivity — waiting until tax season to figure things out. We believe better outcomes come from thoughtful planning and clear communication throughout the year.

Traditional tax work looks backward.
We focus forward.

As a San Diego area CPA firm, we help clients make informed decisions before deadlines, filings, and problems arise. That’s where clarity comes from — and where most tax savings actually happen.

One Strategy, Not Disconnected Advice

Often business owners are forced to deal with fragmented support. They have a bookkeeper who doesn’t interface with the CPA. A CPA who only shows up at tax time. And zero strategy when it comes to making real business decisions.

Our approach centers on one coordinated strategy, led by your CPA, with an integrated financial team aligned around your goals.

How bookkeeping is integrated

Bookkeeping That Supports Your Tax Strategy

Ryan is a licensed bookkeeper as well as a CPA, and he personally handles bookkeeping for a select group of clients where it’s the right fit.

For most engagements, day-to-day bookkeeping is handled by trusted bookkeeping partners who meet our standards for accuracy, communication, and professionalism — with Ryan working closely with every bookkeeper on your account.

Here’s what matters:

  • Your bookkeeper and CPA are aligned
  • Your books support tax planning — not just reporting
  • Issues are identified early, not at year-end
  • You’re not stuck coordinating between providers
  • Ryan stays actively involved, reviewing the numbers, advising on strategy, and ensuring bookkeeping supports your tax goals.

You get the benefit of specialized bookkeeping and proactive tax oversight — without juggling relationships or repeating yourself.
